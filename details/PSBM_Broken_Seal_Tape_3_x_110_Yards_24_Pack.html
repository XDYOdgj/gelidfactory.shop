PSBM Broken Seal Tape

This broken seal tape is designed to provide extra security to your packages. It features a bold red message "STOP If Seal is Broken" printed on white tape, clearly warning customers to inspect the contents before signing for delivery. This helps prevent tampering or theft and ensures complete trust in your shipping process.

Made with industrial-grade 2.0 mil thick acrylic, the tape offers excellent adhesion to both smooth and textured surfaces. Its durable construction ensures long-lasting performance, making it ideal for a variety of use cases such as securing important documents, packaging food, liquor, and more. The standard 3-inch core is compatible with most tape dispensers, offering a convenient and practical solution for everyday packaging needs.

With 24 rolls in a single pack, this product is an economical option for businesses and individuals looking for reliable sealing solutions. It's perfect for shipping, mailing, and inventory management, helping you maintain professionalism and security in every delivery.
