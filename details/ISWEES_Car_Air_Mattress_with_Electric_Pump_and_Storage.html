The ISWEES Car Bed SUV Air Mattress

Transform your car into a cozy sleeping space with the ISWEES Car Bed SUV Air Mattress. Whether you're planning a weekend road trip, a camping adventure, or simply relaxing at the beach, this mattress keeps you comfortable and safe during your travels. Its unique honeycomb structure supports your spine and enhances your sleep experience, making it perfect for both adults and kids.

Key Features

  • The honeycomb structured design provides superior spinal support and maintains comfort for long hours on the road.
  • Double-sided child protection system ensures a secure ride and prevents kids from moving into the front seat while driving.
  • Includes 2 inflatable pillows and 2 footrests for extra support and a more stable sleeping position.
  • Easy to inflate and deflate with an electric pump, taking just 4-6 minutes to prepare for your next adventure.
  • Constructed from durable flocking and PVC material, it's built to last and withstand frequent use.

This universal air mattress is designed to fit most car models, including SUVs and MPVs, making it a versatile and practical choice for families or solo travelers.
