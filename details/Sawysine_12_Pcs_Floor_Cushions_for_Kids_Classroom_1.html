Sawysine 12-Piece Floor Cushions for Kids

These floor cushions are specially crafted to enhance comfort and safety for young learners in classrooms, kindergartens, and homeschool environments. With a soft foam core, they provide gentle support that helps kids sit relaxed for long periods, reducing strain and the risk of hip injuries.

Each cushion features a durable, waterproof PU cover that makes cleaning easy and maintains a fresh, hygienic look. The breathable material ensures comfort throughout the day, while the vibrant pastel colors add a playful, inviting touch that encourages children to choose their preferred seat for studying, drawing, or creative play.

Designed for flexibility and convenience, these square floor cushions are equipped with lightweight handles that let kids carry them effortlessly to their favorite spots. This promotes independence and active learning, making them a perfect choice for educational settings where movement and comfort are essential.

Whether you're looking for classroom seating pads that offer support, or soft foam seat cushions that are easy to maintain, these 12-piece floor cushions provide a versatile, durable, and child-friendly solution for all your learning needs.
