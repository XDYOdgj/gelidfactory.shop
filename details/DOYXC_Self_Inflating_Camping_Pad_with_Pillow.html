DOYXC Self Inflating Camping Pad

Say goodbye to the hassle of carrying separate air pumps and hello to effortless setup with DOYXC's self-inflating camping pad. This innovative design comes with a built-in air pump, making inflation and deflation a breeze and ensuring you can enjoy a comfortable sleep in no time.

Ultra-Thick Memory Foam Comfort

Our camping pad is crafted with premium 28D memory foam that conforms to your body, eliminating pressure points and providing a restful night's sleep whether you're nestled in the woods or relaxing at the campsite.

Perfect for All Seasons

Designed with an R-value of 9.5, this camping pad keeps you warm during cold winter nights and cool in the summer heat. Its breathability and moisture resistance make it a reliable choice for any outdoor adventure.

Lightweight and Compact

With a compact design that compresses over 50% when deflated, this pad saves valuable space in your camping gear. Its lightweight construction means you can easily carry it without adding unnecessary bulk to your pack.

Featuring a built-in pillow for head support, this camping mattress combines comfort, support, and quiet performance. The durable 50D elastic fabric ensures it withstands rough outdoor conditions and maintains its shape over time.

Whether you're hiking through the wilderness, camping in your SUV, or simply traveling by car, this versatile camping pad is the ultimate solution for a restful and enjoyable outdoor experience. It's ideal for solo or family trips, designed to make your adventures more comfortable without the hassle of extra setup.
